Assume $p,q \ge 1$. Consider the transformation of $\phi(B)X_t = \theta(B)X_t$. 

\[
W_t = 
\begin{cases} 
  \sigma^{-1}X_t & t \in \{1, \ldots, \max(p,q) \} \\
  \sigma^{-1}\phi(B)X_t & t > \max(p,q) 
\end{cases}
\]

We apply the Innovations Algorithm to this.

\end{frame}
